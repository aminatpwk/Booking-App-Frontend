Human Rights Statement

Our Commitment

The Booking App project and its associated development team (Amina Sokoli and 3i Solutions Albania) are committed to respecting and promoting fundamental human rights as outlined in the Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR).

We strive to ensure that our operations, technologies, and products do not directly or indirectly infringe upon the rights of individuals or communities.

Non-Discrimination and Equal Access

We believe in equal opportunity and non-discrimination. Our application is designed to be accessible to all users, regardless of their background, gender, religion, race, national origin, or other protected characteristics.

  • Accessibility: We prioritize the development of accessible interfaces to ensure that individuals with disabilities can effectively use our service.
  • Fair Treatment: We require that all service providers utilizing our platform adhere to non-discriminatory booking practices.

Privacy and Data Rights

Respecting the right to privacy is a core principle. We are committed to protecting the personal data of our users and ensuring transparency in our data handling practices, as detailed in our Privacy Policy.

  • Consent: We seek clear and informed consent for data collection and processing.
  • Security: We utilize robust security measures (e.g., encryption) to protect user data from unauthorized access or breaches.
  • Transparency: Users have the right to know what data is collected and how it is used.

Freedom of Expression

We support the right to freedom of expression and opinion. We aim to provide an open platform while balancing this right with the need to maintain a safe and legal environment, prohibiting content that promotes hate, discrimination, or illegal acts.

Reporting and Accountability

We are committed to continuous improvement and accountability. If a user or service provider believes the Booking App is failing to uphold these principles, we encourage prompt reporting.
