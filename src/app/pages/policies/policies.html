The Booking App is a project developed by Amina Sokoli as part of an internship at 3i Solutions Albania, dedicated to creating efficient reservation systems.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide and improve the service. This may include:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and password (encrypted) provided during registration or booking.
  • Reservation Details: Date, time, service requested, and service provider used.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the purpose of managing reservations and improving our service:

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
