Simplifying Your Reservations

Our Mission and Vision

At Booking App, our core mission is to streamline the reservation process for both service providers and customers. We believe that booking an appointment, a table, or a service should be fast, reliable, and entirely stress-free. Our vision is to become the leading reservation platform in the region, known for its intuitive design and robust performance.

Built with Passion and Precision

This application is the result of a dedicated internship project at 3i Solutions Albania, a modern software development and IT consulting company. We were challenged to apply the latest technologies, like Angular, to create a practical, effective, and user-centric product from the ground up. This project showcases 3i Solutions Albania's commitment to finding efficient systems and developing reliable software solutions.

The Technology Behind It

The Booking App is built using the Angular framework for a fast, dynamic front-end experience. We prioritize clean code, modular design, and accessibility to ensure every user has a seamless experience, whether they are booking on a desktop or a mobile device.
